The Mythos of Sortaea

In the early ages...

There it was, a spark fading over sticks and leaves failing to start the flames. The very flames needed to survive another night in this cold and dark world. War-torn and slave infested, pestilence and disease scratched the surface of torment for so many. However, once every hundred years or so a few heroes would be born to rise against the torment of this world. Though, a light to make the darkness fade is a light that empowers shadows.

..As time went on..

Order is maintained and the generations of heroes that are immortalized maintain the balance of good and evil under the strict law of Primus, who directly serves The Dimensional Architect. The world was not so dark or cold anymore. The evil that once flourished grow hungry. They grow powerful, influenced and encouraged by the light that doesn’t fade and to keep growing and plotting until all of their intentions to reign supreme are all but insured.

...And now

It’s up to a new group of heroes to grow under the light and guidance of their elder generations to overpower the evil that awaits. Though, these heroes are destined for great things, that does not mean they aren’t destined for the betterment of life. Only the future to come will hold what is in store for these new heroes. Well, to call them heroes would be to assume that evil will not tempt them to sacrifice the light for their own desires.
